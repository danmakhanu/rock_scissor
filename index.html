🎮 Rock Paper Scissors

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game! Challenge the computer in a five-round match and see who comes out on top.

How to Play

  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock.

Game Rules

  1. You will play against the computer.
  2. The game consists of 5 rounds.
  3. In each round, enter Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  4. The winner of each round earns one point.
  5. The player with the highest score after 5 rounds wins.
